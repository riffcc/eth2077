### Reuse potential

Strong reusable primitives exist in Citadel/Lagoon for:

- diff-sync and dissemination,
- transport/mobility,
- topology-informed consensus components.

### Blocking gaps for "formally verified Ethereum client"

- Missing Ethereum-native execution/client surfaces in current Citadel/Lagoon codebases.
- Placeholder and assumption debt remains significant in formal artifacts.
- Several runtime paths are partially implemented or marked TODO.
